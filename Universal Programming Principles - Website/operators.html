Operators


What are Operators?

Comparision Operators are symbols used to compare different values. They are also used
in programming to form Boolean expressions (expessions that only have two possible answers;
true and false). Operators can also be used in Conditional Statements.


Here are a few examples of common operators used in programming:

Operator; Description;
> is greater than
< is less than
>= is greater than or equal to
<= is less than or equal to
== is equal to
!= is not equal to
